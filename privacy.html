Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TK Growth LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with the OMPC mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

When you use OMPC, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Your email address, display name (which may differ from your legal name), and login credentials (stored in encrypted form).
  • Activity logs: Records of activities you log within the app, including type, date, and any associated details you choose to enter.
  • Messages: Communications you send to other users within the app.

We do not collect your real name, phone number, location data, or device identifiers unless you choose to provide them.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the OMPC app and its features
  • Enable accountability connections between users
  • Deliver messages between users within the app
  • Maintain and improve the app's performance and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

Data Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database and authentication service. Supabase stores data on secure cloud infrastructure. By using OMPC, you acknowledge that your data will be processed and stored by Supabase in accordance with their privacy practices. For more information, visit supabase.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with advertisers or marketing partners. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (such as Supabase for data storage), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate notice to users.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account through the app, your data is removed from our active systems. Residual copies in backups may persist for up to 90 days before being purged in the ordinary course of our backup rotation schedule.

Group Data

OMPC is a group-based app. The following data behaviors apply when you participate in or leave a group:

  • If you leave a group that continues to exist: Your activity logs and messages posted in that group remain in the app and are visible to remaining group members. You cannot delete this data after leaving a group without deleting your account entirely.
  • If you are the last member to leave a group: The group is dissolved and all associated activity logs and messages are permanently deleted.
  • If you delete your account: All of your data across all groups is permanently deleted.

By participating in a group, you acknowledge and consent to these data retention behaviors.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app. Once deleted, this action cannot be undone.
  • Correction: You can update your display name and email address within your account settings.

Children's Privacy

OMPC is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.
